Flow of our app

Note: Here each tab defines about each container in home page of our app

View Tracker

Through this the mentor can view the tracker filled by the volunteer, and after the volunteer withdrawn , the mentor get access to fill the tracker

Review form

The mentor should give feedback for the relationship with children based on the holistic indicators based on which the graph will be generated

Graph

The mentor can view the graph of holistic indicators for the connected children filled by mentor and volunteer

View Profile

The Mentor can able to view its own details which are entered by admin through this tab

View Volunteer Profile

The Mentor can able to view the details of the volunteer connected to the child through this tab

View child Profile

The Mentor can able to view the details of the child connected to the child through this tab

Contact us

The mentor can able to connect with the admin team

Donate

Mentor can able to donate and support the process
