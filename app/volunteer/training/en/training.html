Flow of our app

Note: Here each tab defines about each container in home page of our app

Select Child

The volunteer can able to select the child based on his interest from the listed child. After the confirmation of admin, the tab will be replaced by children profile, or the volunteer should again select another child

Select Mentor

The volunteer can able to select the mentor for the connected child from the list of the mentor. After the confirmation of admin, the tab will be replaced by mentor profile, or the volunteer should again select another mentor

Tracker

The volunteer can fill the tracker and holistic indicators for the generation of graph

View Tracker

Through this the volunteer can view the tracker filled by the volunteer, and after the volunteer withdrawn , the mentor get access to fill the tracker

Graph

The Volunteer can view the graph of holistic indicators for the connected children filled by mentor and volunteer

View Profile

The Volunteer can able to view its own details which are entered by admin through this tab

View Mentor Profile

The Volunteer can able to view the details of the mentor connected to the child through this tab

View child Profile

The volunteer can able to view the details of the child connected to the child through this tab

Contact us

The Volunteer can able to connect with the admin team

Donate

Volunteer can able to donate and support the process
