Flow of our app

Note: Here each tab defines about each container in home page of our app

Pre - Assessment Form

When the child login for the first time, it displays the pre assessment form where the basic details and goal related details, family and financial condition, likes and dislikes of the child are obtained

View Pre-Assessment form

After the Pre-Assessment form is filled, the child can able to view the Pre-Assessment form through this functionality.

Review form

The child can fill the review form for both the volunteer and mentor to assess the process

View Profile

The child can able to view its own details which are entered by admin through this tab

View Volunteer Profile

The child can able to view the details of the volunteer connected to the child through this tab

View Mentor Profile

The child can able to view the details of the volunteer connected to the child through this tab

View Graph

The child can able to view the graph for the holistic indicator filled by the volunteer and mentor

Contact us

The child can able to connect with the admin team

About us

The child can able to view the details of the Organization and the process of N'KaNa
